Company Bio

Three ladies trying to make Austin a little greener. reAUSTIN, where you can find and plot recycling bins, or if you don't fill yours up often, put your personal one up for use. If you're looking for a place to recycle, the flags mark public centers and the pins mark places added by other users. If you'd like to contribute, click anywhere on the contribute map and then click on your new pin to add information.
